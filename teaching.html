About

Danfeng Shan (单丹枫) is an assistant professor at School of Computer Science and Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He received his Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2018, and the B.E. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2013. His research interests include data center networking and congestion control.
